FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Can I change the weight unit?
Yes. Both kg and lb are supported in Settings.
How do I delete a log?
Open the record date and swipe left on the workout list.
What is iCloud backup?
It keeps your logs in iCloud so they transfer with device changes.
I can’t find a workout to log.
Please contact us. Open the request form.
How do I add a workout to favorites?
Select a workout in the Workouts tab and tap the ☆ icon.
