Log workouts without hesitation

Biggr is a simple, easy-to-use workout log app.
You can log from the calendar in seconds, review with a single tap, and save favorite workouts. iCloud sync makes it easy to carry your data over when you switch devices.

Features

Designed so you can understand each step at a glance.

Log from the calendar

Pick a date and start logging in seconds.

Review instantly

See past workouts at a glance and track changes.

Search and save workouts

Keep favorites handy to shorten input.

Trust & privacy

Your training data stays simple and transparent.

  • Your data is stored on your device and iCloud. We do not send it to our own servers.
  • iCloud sync can be toggled on or off in iOS Settings.
  • See details in the Privacy Policy.

FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Can I change the weight unit?
Yes. Both kg and lb are supported in Settings.
How do I delete a log?
Open the record date and swipe left on the workout list.
What is iCloud backup?
It keeps your logs in iCloud so they transfer with device changes.
I can’t find a workout to log.
Please contact us. Open the request form.
How do I add a workout to favorites?
Select a workout in the Workouts tab and tap the ☆ icon.
