Settings

  • Can I changeweight units?

    Yes.Both kg and lbare supported,and you can switch unitsin Settings.

Logging

  • How do I deletea logged record?

    In the Log tab,select the date you want,then swipe lefton the workout entryto delete it.

Backup

  • What isiCloud backup?

    It saves your data to iCloudso you can restore your logsif you switch devicesor lose your phone.See Apple’s official guidehere.

  • Can I back upmy data?

    iCloud sync providesautomatic backups.To opt out, go toiPhone Settings → Apple ID →iCloud → Apps Using iCloudand turn off Biggr.

Workouts

  • I can't finda workout to log.

    Please contact uswith the details.Open the request form.

  • How do I add a workoutto favorites?

    Open the Workouts tab,select the workout you want,and tap the ☆ iconin the top right.
